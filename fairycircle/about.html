We are the Writers. (She/Her/He/Him)
You may also call us Ms. Briar Rose.

This is a vent location: we will write here, as well as vent here. Explanations for anything written here may never arrive

Never having found a safe space, we've used the internet to vent out frustrations we've had with life for an exorbitant amount of time. We've been through therapy many times, tried to come up with better coping mechanisms, and none of it has worked. At this time, the family is not safe to talk to concerning mental health, so we have turned here. The internet is home to many types of sites from different walks of life, so there is no harm in sharing certain things here. It's not the safest place in the world, but it can be a place to talk about problems and coping mechanisms without much judgement.

Choosing here over real life is depressing, isn't it? I wholeheartedly agree.

I advise that you avoid this place if you are easily triggered, because unlike up there, there is no filter here, and there never will be.
